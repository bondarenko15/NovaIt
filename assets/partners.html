Genesys is a leading global provider of solutions for contact centers and customer experience (CX) management. Founded in 1990, the company specializes in creating innovative platforms that enable businesses to effectively engage with their customers through various communication channels. Its solutions are designed to deliver personalized service based on advanced technologies such as cloud services, artificial intelligence (AI), and analytics.
