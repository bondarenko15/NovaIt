TSB Bank Limited (TSB) is a 100% New Zealand-owned community bank with 25 branches nationwide. Delivering empathetic and superior customer experience is at the heart of TSB. In 2019, it recorded a customer satisfaction score of 81.3% — the highest of any bank in New Zealand.
